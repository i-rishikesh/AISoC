About AISoC

AISoC is the Society for excellence in Artificial Intelligence based in KIIT Bhubaneswar. It is a community of students supported by Prof. Murari Mandal and Prof. Vikas Hassija School of Computer Engineering KIIT Who are passionate to learn, educate, and develop projects in the emerging fields of Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. AISoC aims to provide opportunities for students enthusiastic about learning discussing, and applying AI in different fields of study.
