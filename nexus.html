Food Nutritient Identification App

Objective:
We are usually not conscious about the amount of nutritients present in our daily meals. A Nutrient-Aware Application is a software tool designed to help individuals track and manage their daily nutrient intake. The primary goal of such an application is to promote a healthy and balanced diet by providing users with accurate and relevant information about the nutrients present in the food they consume.

The application can use an ML/DL model trained on a image database of foods, which includes the nutritional information for each food item such as calorie count, macronutrient breakdown (carbohydrates, proteins, and fats), vitamins, and minerals. Users can input the image of the food they are about to consume, and the application will calculate the total nutrient intake for that meal

    Use Case Scenarios:
  1. Click a picture of your plate and upload in the App.
  2. The App should first detect the types of dishes present in that plate. (Object Detection or Semantic Segmentation)
  3. Quantity of each Dish in the plate (Image based quantity (ml/gms) prediction)
  4. Create an Excel Sheet where the individual Nutrient component for each dish is given. How much of each nutrient is available in this particular dish is also reported.
  5. Create a Chart/Graph for visualization

Optional:
The application may also include features such as personalized nutrition plans, reminders to drink water, and goals for daily nutrient intake based on factors such as age, weight, height, and activity level. Some applications may also offer recipe suggestions and allow users to track their progress over time. Benefits? In addition to individual use, the application can be useful for healthcare professionals, nutritionists, and dietitians to monitor the nutrient intake of their patients and clients. In conclusion, a Nutrient-Aware Application is a valuable tool for promoting a healthy diet and lifestyle. With its accurate and comprehensive information, it helps individuals make informed decisions about their nutritional intake and achieve their health and wellness goals.

NFT based student attendance system

Objective:
The objective is to create NFT or smart contract-based gamification system is to improve the process of taking student attendance by making it more efficient and fair, while addressing the issue of proxy attendance. The game needs to be designed in a way that no student is motivated to apply proxy for any other student. It should be an open network where students can mention if someone is absent. The rules should be such that if someone is not present in the class and yet he is marking himself present online, others should help the system to catch him. Those who help the system to give fair attendance will get rewarded and those who try to cheat the system will get penalized.

    Use Case Scenarios:
  1. Record attendance in traditional classrooms and online/virtual classes.
  2. Click a picture of your prescription and upload in the App.
  3. Provide teachers and administrators with an accurate, real-time record of student attendance.
  4. Incentivize consistent attendance through a reward system for students with high attendance.
  5. Track attendance as a factor in determining academic performance.
  6. Those who have cheated ,points will be deducted.
Optional:

Receive alerts when students have missed a certain number of classes, allowing teachers and administrators to intervene and support students. Benefit? The benefits includes increased accuracy, reduced time and effort, improved attendance, enhanced transparency, student engagement, data-driven decision making, and increased accountability. This system eliminates human error, saves time and effort for teachers, encourages students to attend classes, provides a transparent and accountable system, and uses data to drive decisions.

Reducing Idling at Traffic Lights

Objective:

To create an algorithm with a sleek UI that aims to reduce idling time and carbon emissions at signalized intersections by using autonomous vehicles with AI-controlled speed in a simulated environment.

    Use Case Scenarios:
  1. This will be a stimulation based project where there will be a hypothetical map with traffic lights at various intersections.
  2. Each traffic light will be running with it's own cycle and each cycle will unique for each light.
  3. The objective of this algorithm will be to determine the speed of the vehicle at which the it will encounter maximum amount of green lights from point A to point B which can be determined by the user
    Optional:
  1. Adding vehicles with different speeds and latencies so as to emulate real-life traffic
  2. Collection and analysis of real-time traffic data to continuously improve the AI algorithms. (Make this algorithm self-improving if possible.)
  3. User-friendly interface for traffic management officials to monitor and control the system.
    Benefits:
  1. Improved air quality by reducing vehicle emissions from idling engines.
  2. Reduced fuel consumption and associated cost savings for drivers.
  3. Improved traffic flow and reduced congestion.
  4. Increased road safety by reducing the likelihood of vehicles accelerating suddenly when the light turns green.
  5. Better use of public infrastructure and resources with a projected reduction of at least 25% in idling time and carbon emissions.
